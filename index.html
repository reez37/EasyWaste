PEOPLE DUMP WASTE ON STREETS
WHY???

Current systems in India cannot cope with the volumes of waste generated by an increasing urban population, and this impacts on the environment and public health. India faces major environmental challenges associated with waste generation and inadequate waste collection, transport, treatment and disposal.

We have observed that the waste management in our country is underdeveloped and the waste disposal service is made available only for a couple of time over week.
But if we are not on time we have to wait another two or more days for them to come and collect our waste or people have to dump it in some random place or public dustbins which results in a negative impact on our surrounding places.
Dumping wastes on random places brings a serious health hazard, it will lead to many diseases and other negative impacts.
It is very important to keep our place, surroundings and environment clean and also a s a part of swatch Bharat, a proper method of waste management has to be induced to keep our country clean and healthy.
